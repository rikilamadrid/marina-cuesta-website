Driven by a vocation for powerful ideas, recognized as one of the most influential female voices in advertising.

For more than twenty years I've led integrated platforms and campaigns across North American, U.S. Hispanic and European markets — for clients including Apple, McDonald's, GOYA Foods, Chevrolet, FritoLay and Sony. My path runs through TBWA, CHINA, Dieste and The Marketing Arm, and most recently launching Creyentes / We Believers in the United States.

I lead with empathy, inspiration and purpose, collaborating across countries and cultures. I'm a committed advocate for creative mentorship, with a particular focus on fostering diversity, inclusion and the women who are reshaping this industry.

A lover of cultures and new adventures, I find joy in travel — often alongside my greatest creative project: my family.
